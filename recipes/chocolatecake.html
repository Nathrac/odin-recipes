One Bowl Chocolate Cake

One Bowl Chocolate Cakerecipe from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3⁄4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3⁄4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1⁄2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans
  3. Stir sugar, fluor, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer.
  5. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.
  6. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rak to cool completely.
  8. Enjoy your delicious chocolate cake!
