Key Lime Pie

Easy Key Lime Pie recipe from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 5 beaten egg yolks
  • 1 (14 ounce) can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1⁄2 cup key lime juice
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  3. Combine sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and egg yolks in a large bowl; mix well.
  4. Pour mixture into unbaked graham cracker crust.
  5. Baked in th preheated oven until filling sets. This should take around 15 minutes.
  6. Allow to cool completely before slicing and serve.
